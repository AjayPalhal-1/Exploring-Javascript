




Key Takeaways for Interviews

1. for...in  :   Used for objects to iterate over keys.
2. for...of  :   Used for arrays or strings to iterate over values.
3. forEach   :    Array-specific method, executes a callback for every element.

4. Difference:


for...in    gives keys,
for...of    gives values,
forEach     is for arrays only, allowing direct execution of a function.
